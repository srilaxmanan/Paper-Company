Faucet Aerators: These are small devices attached to faucets that mix air with water, reducing water flow while maintaining a full stream and minimizing splashing. Lawn Aerators: These tools are used to create holes in the soil to allow air, water, and nutrients to reach the grass roots, improving soil health and promoting better lawn growth. Wastewater Treatment Aerators: These devices are used to introduce oxygen into wastewater to aid in the breakdown of organic matter by microorganisms.
